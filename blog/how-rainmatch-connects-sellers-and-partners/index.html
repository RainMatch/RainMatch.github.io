How RainMatch Connects Sellers and Partners

One of the most common questions we hear from new users is: how does the matching actually work? RainMatch isn't a directory you browse through manually — it's a curated system designed to surface the right connections at the right time. Here's a look at what happens behind the scenes.

The Two Sides of Every Deal

Every successful deal on RainMatch starts with two distinct roles: Sellers and Partners.

  • Sellers are professionals or business owners who have a product or service to sell and want help expanding their reach. They post Projects describing what they offer, their ideal customer profile, and the commission they're willing to share.
  • Partners are experienced professionals — consultants, industry veterans, or well-connected executives — who bring credibility, relationships, and domain expertise that can help Sellers close deals.

Many users naturally fit both roles and can switch between them depending on the opportunity.

Curated Matching, Not Endless Scrolling

Rather than presenting you with an overwhelming list of people to cold-contact, RainMatch curates a small set of high-quality matches daily. This approach is intentional. Quality over quantity is the core principle.

The matching system considers several factors:

  1. Industry and domain expertise — matching Partners with genuine knowledge in a Seller's market segment.
  2. Network relevance — assessing whether a Partner's connections align with the Seller's ideal customer profile.
  3. Past performance on RainMatch — rewarding users who consistently close deals and maintain positive collaboration history.
  4. Deal preferences and commission expectations — ensuring that both parties enter conversations with aligned incentives.

Mutual Interest Creates the Connection

A match only becomes a real connection when both sides express interest. Sellers browse suggested Partners and can like profiles they find promising. Partners browse curated Projects and like the ones they want to pursue. When both parties like each other, a connection is made and the collaboration can begin.

This mutual-interest gate filters out mismatched outreach and ensures that every conversation starts with genuine intent from both sides.

From Match to Closed Deal

Once connected, Sellers and Partners discuss the specifics of their collaboration directly within the app. They agree on commission structures — which can be percentage-based or a fixed fee — and begin working together to engage the Seller's target customers.

As deals close, both parties build their reputation on the platform. Strong track records unlock enhanced visibility, giving top performers greater exposure to future opportunities.

The RainMatch matching system is constantly improving. If you have feedback on your experience or suggestions for how we can surface better matches, we'd love to hear from you at rainmaker@rainmatch.ai.
