Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RainMatch, the curated business partner matching platform.

Getting Started

What is RainMatch? +
RainMatch is a curated matchmaking and collaboration platform for sales and business professionals. It connects Sellers (people with products or services to sell) with Partners (experienced professionals who have the right network, credibility, or expertise to help close deals). When deals close, commissions are shared—creating a true win-win model.
Who is RainMatch for? +
RainMatch is designed for:
  • Sales and business development professionals and leaders
  • Business owners & founders
  • Consultants & industry experts
If you sell products or services—or have valuable relationships that can help others sell—RainMatch is built for you.
What's the difference between a Seller and a Partner? +
Sellers post projects for products or services they want help selling.

Partners bring industry expertise, relationships, and credibility to help Sellers close deals.

Many users can act as both Seller and Partner, depending on the opportunity.
How do I get started? +
Getting started takes just a few minutes:
  • Sign up using LinkedIn, Google, Apple or email
  • Choose your role – Seller or Partner (you can add the other role later in your Profile)
  • Complete your profile – the more details you provide, the higher the chances of getting accurate matches
  • Post a project (Sellers)
  • Explore matches, discover Projects and Partners, chat with them

Matching & Connections

How does matching work? +
RainMatch uses a carefully curated smart matching system that considers:
  • Industry and domain expertise
  • Network relevance
  • Past success history on RainMatch
  • Deal preferences and commission expectations
Matches are curated daily to ensure quality over quantity. A connection only happens when both parties express interest.
How many matches do I get per day? +
It varies but theoretically it's unlimited. You are more likely to get more matches if you share your LinkedIn URL and provide as much detail as you can in your profile.
How many likes can I submit per day? +
Free Plan:
  • Sellers can like up to 3 Partner matches per day
  • Partners can like up to 5 Seller projects per day
Pro Plan:
  • Sellers can like up to 10 Partner matches per day
  • Partners can like up to 15 Seller projects per day
Premium Plan with unlimited likes and other advanced features is coming soon. Partners on Pro Plan or higher are eligible to earn the "Rainmaker" badge based on their Seller connections, project successes, revenue generated, commissions earned, and success stories shared.
RainMatch is currently free for everyone with unlimited features!
How do I know I can trust the people I work with? +
RainMatch uses multiple trust and credibility signals, including:
  • Verified professional profiles (LinkedIn & Google)
  • Ratings and reviews after each collaboration
  • Deal completion history
  • Success stories and reputation badges
Over time, top performers earn recognition as Pro/Premium Sellers or Pro/Premium Partners.

Commissions & Pricing

How do commissions work? +
Commissions are agreed upon before collaboration begins. Sellers can define in their Projects:
  • Percentage-based commissions (based on revenue or profit)
  • Fixed fees
RainMatch does not engage in Seller-Partner negotiation and agreement.
Coming soon: A standardized digital contract will be available on RainMatch to facilitate seamless contract execution.
Does RainMatch take a cut of my commissions? +
RainMatch does not engage in Seller-Partner negotiation and agreement, and primarily operates on user subscription plans.
Is RainMatch free to use? +
Yes! RainMatch offers a Free plan for both Sellers and Partners. Paid plans unlock additional match likes, advanced profile listing such as URLs and documents, enhanced/featured visibility, advanced analytics, and contract management capabilities.
RainMatch is currently free for everyone with unlimited features!
What are the paid plans? +
For Sellers:
  • Free
  • Pro – $49/month
For Partners:
  • Free
  • Pro – $29/month
Exact features may evolve as we launch new capabilities.
RainMatch is currently free for everyone with unlimited features!
Are Seller-Partner contracts built into the platform? +
Not yet, but it is coming soon! RainMatch will be adding built-in electronic contracts so Sellers and Partners can formalize agreements quickly and securely—without needing external tools.

Platform & Features

What platforms does RainMatch support? +
RainMatch is built as a mobile-first platform with:
  • Native iOS and Android apps
Coming soon: A web dashboard for deeper project and deal management, and analytics.
Is RainMatch suitable for enterprise B2B sales? +
Absolutely! Although RainMatch is not limited to enterprise sales or any particular industry, it can be especially powerful for:
  • Enterprise B2B sales
  • SaaS and technology products
  • Industry-specific or regulated markets
  • High-ticket, relationship-driven deals
RainMatch is built for any industry where there are Sellers and Partners to collaborate. The opportunity is unlimited.
How is RainMatch different from LinkedIn or traditional networking? +
Unlike traditional networking platforms, RainMatch:
  • Focuses on closing deals, not just making connections
  • Aligns incentives through commission-based collaboration
  • Curates matches instead of overwhelming you with leads
  • Offers a fun and simple user interface
Is my data secure? +
Yes. RainMatch is built with enterprise-grade security, secure authentication, and privacy controls. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
