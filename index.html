What is RainMatch?

RainMatch is a platform for salespeople to connect with the right people for two goals.

As a Seller, if you need help selling your product, find the right Partners to help you win deals.

As a Partner, if you have the network and customers in certain industries and are willing to offer help, connect with the right Sellers, and help them win deals.

Who is RainMatch for?

Sellers

Sales professionals, business owners, and entrepreneurs who have products or services to sell but need expanded networks, industry expertise, and additional credibility to close more deals faster.

Partners

Experienced sales professionals, consultants, industry veterans, and business development experts who possess valuable networks, relationships, and domain expertise that can help Sellers win more deals in exchange for commission sharing.
